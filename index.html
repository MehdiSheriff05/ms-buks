About Us

MS Buks is a fictional family-owned bookstore based in Mauritius. Built on a love for reading, learning, and community, the store offers a carefully selected collection of novels, fantasy books, children’s stories, classics, and educational titles. Our aim is to create a warm and welcoming place where every reader can discover something meaningful.
